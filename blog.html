Post 6: Embracing AI Tools as a Learning Companion in Frontend Development

📅Published on: June 2026

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has significantly transformed how students learn programming. Throughout this individual project, utilizing AI tools as a coding assistant helped me understand complex CSS layouts and debug structural HTML errors much faster than conventional methods.

However, the key to using AI in software development is not to let it do all the work, but to treat it as a supportive peer. It helps bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical execution, allowing informatics students to focus more on creativity, logic, and unique user interface innovations.
